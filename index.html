About SCOME

What is
SCOME?

The Standing Committee on Medical Education (SCOME) is one of the oldest Standing Committees in IFMSA — the International Federation of Medical Students' Associations — established in 1951.

EKSUMSA SCOME is the local chapter at Ekiti State University, Ado-Ekiti, committed to elevating the quality of medical education for every student in our institution and beyond.

SCOME operates across 38 IFMSA National Member Organizations worldwide, and 6 of 26 NMOs in Africa — we are proud to be part of that growing African voice.

SCOME
Our Foundation

Vision &
Mission

Vision Statement

Medical students are meaningfully engaged within their respective Medical Education Systems and represent student voice in issues regarding Medical Education as full-fledged stakeholders on all levels in order to create quality Medical Education worldwide and attain suitable competencies which will enable them to answer the healthcare needs of communities they have a mandate to serve.

Mission Statement

SCOME is a platform that creates a shared environment where medical students contribute, reflect and work on the development of Medical Education through student-led activities. SCOME enables medical students to reach their full potential as Medical Education leaders through activities aimed at education, research, capacity development, meaningful youth engagement and advocacy in order to create effective impact on local, national, regional and international level.
