Community Engagement

Outreach Focus Areas

Health Advocacy Campaigns
Running awareness campaigns tied to global health days — World TB Day, World Diabetes Day, World AIDS Day, World Mental Health Day, and more across the academic year.
Peer Education & Secondary Schools
Taking medical knowledge into secondary schools through health education workshops, career talks about medicine, and early exposure to clinical thinking for younger students.
Digital Education Initiatives
Partnering with e-learning platforms to widen access to quality digital medical content, building a culture of tech-enhanced learning within EKSU College of Medicine.
Student Welfare & Mental Health
Support groups, burnout prevention webinars, and mental health resource distribution — because the wellbeing of every medical student matters as much as academic excellence.
